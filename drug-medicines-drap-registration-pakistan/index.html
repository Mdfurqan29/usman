  • Trademark Registration of Pharmaceuticals / Medicine Products in Pakistan

In this modern era, the healthcare wants of the people are changing at a fast pace. Therefore, it is essential for health care companies to register their medicines or biological drugs in Pakistan. In this way, customers will be able to trust the pharmaceutical company with their health care needs. There are two ways, you can get your pharmaceutical or medicine product registered in Pakistan. The first way is to get your DRUG license registered from DRAP (Drug Regulatory Authority In Pakistan). A drug license from DRAP is not necessary to register the name of your Pharma Product. Whereas, the second way which is recommended is to get it registered from IPO (Intellectual Property Organization of Pakistan) NAZ Intellectual Services Pakistan offers their expert consultation on registration of your Herbal, Organic, Nutraceutical, and Pharmaceutical Medicines in Pakistan.
